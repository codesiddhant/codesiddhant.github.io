Privacy & Data Policy

Your Privacy Matters

At RAuth OneID, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Data Collection

We collect only essential information needed to provide our services:

  • Mobile number for authentication
  • Basic profile information (name, date of birth, gender)
  • KYC documents for identity verification
  • Location data for security monitoring
  • Device information for session management

Data Usage

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • Authenticating your identity across partner apps
  • Providing security alerts and monitoring
  • Complying with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Improving our services

Data Protection

We employ industry-standard security measures:

  • End-to-end encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure storage with military-grade encryption
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Zero-knowledge architecture where possible

Data Sharing

We never share your personal data without your explicit consent. When you approve a login or KYC request, only the necessary information is shared with that specific app.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request data correction or deletion
  • Revoke app permissions at any time
  • Export your data
  • Close your account

What is Pre-KYC?

One KYC, Infinite Possibilities

Pre-KYC is a revolutionary approach to identity verification that saves you time and eliminates repetitive paperwork.

The Traditional Problem

Typically, every app or service requires you to:

  • Submit KYC documents separately
  • Wait for verification each time
  • Store sensitive documents on multiple platforms
  • Repeat the process for every new service

The OneID Solution

With RAuth OneID's Pre-KYC:

  • Complete KYC once: Submit your documents to OneID only
  • Instant verification: Partner apps access your verified status
  • Controlled sharing: You approve what data each app sees
  • Secure storage: Your documents stay encrypted in one place

How It Works

Step 1: Upload Once

Submit your KYC documents (Aadhaar, PAN, Passport) to OneID

Step 2: Get Verified

Our team verifies your documents following regulatory standards

Step 3: Approve Requests

When apps need KYC, approve their request with a single tap

Step 4: Instant Access

Apps receive your verified status instantly - no waiting!

Benefits

  • ⏱️ Save hours of repetitive paperwork
  • 🔒 Enhanced security with centralized storage
  • ✅ Instant verification across all partner apps
  • 🎯 Full control over data sharing
  • 📱 Mobile-first, paperless experience

Security & Compliance

OneID's Pre-KYC system is fully compliant with:

  • Reserve Bank of India (RBI) guidelines
  • Know Your Customer (KYC) regulations
  • Digital Personal Data Protection Act
  • ISO 27001 security standards
